Interactive Visualization of the US Housing prices: Single Family House

Through this interactive visualization, witness the rise and fall of housing prices during each crisis and gain valuable insights into the factors that triggered these market upheavals. Engage with dynamic charts, explore state-wise trends, and uncover the lasting effects of these economic downturns on the nation's real estate landscape. Join us on this data-driven expedition that paints a vivid picture of the housing market's resilience and transformation amidst the challenges of the past two decades.

The narrative visualization depicting the US housing prices crash by recession serves as a poignant reminder of the cyclical nature of the economy. The story serves as a beacon of hope, showing that even in the face of adversity, the American dream of homeownership endures, standing strong against the winds of uncertainty.
